The Evening

More than a meal

01

The Room

Forty seats. Three seatings per evening. Designed by Studio Koto as a space where darkness becomes texture and silence becomes presence.

02

The Kitchen

An open kitchen at the heart of the dining room. Watch the team work in the same light you eat in. No curtain. No division.

03

The Cellar

2,400 labels selected by sommelier Ikaika Nakoa, with a focus on small producers, natural ferments, and wines that have something to say.

04

Private Dining

The Vault seats up to twelve for exclusive tastings, custom menus, and private celebrations. Inquire for availability and pricing.
