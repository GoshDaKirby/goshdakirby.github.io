Fitness Rewards & Social Platform — UI Concept
A mobile-first UI concept for a fitness app that rewards users for working out and staying active. Features include achievement tracking, sponsor deal integrations, and social interactions between users — designed to make fitness feel less like a chore and more like a game.

Design Process
01
Research
Analyzed existing fitness apps — identifying gaps in motivation, retention, and reward systems that most platforms fail to address.
02
Concept
Defined the core loop: exercise → earn points → unlock rewards and sponsor deals → share with friends. Gamification as the hook, real value as the retention.
03
Wireframe
Laid out information architecture and user flows in Figma — mapping every screen transition before touching visual design.
04
Visual Design
Applied a bold, energetic visual language with high contrast, clear hierarchy, and motion-forward UI elements suited to an active user context.
05
Prototype
Built an interactive Figma prototype with full screen-to-screen transitions, tap targets, and simulated flows — viewable without any account or install.
